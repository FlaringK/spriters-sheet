Spriter's Exporter

* Made by FlaringK

* Heya, this tool is made for generating transparent uniform sprite sheets from the sheets on spriter's resouce.
* The sheets over there are pretty diverse however, so this tool is made to be flexible. However there are limitations.
* This tool is made to recgonise sprites with a sprite backing colour, diffrent from the background colour.
* The example to the right is one such sheet.
Upload your image and change your options, then click [Generate Spritesheet]
